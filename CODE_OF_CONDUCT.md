# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Use welcoming language, respect differing viewpoints, accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for the community, and prioritize safety when discussing electrical hardware.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, insults, trolling, publishing private information, or unsafe instructions presented as tested facts are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's security contact. Maintainers may remove, edit, reject, or otherwise act on contributions or comments that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

This concise policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
